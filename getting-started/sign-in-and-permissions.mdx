## When to use this

Use this page immediately after installing Voxas, or anytime permissions need to be re-granted.

<Note>
  Image placeholder: Voxas sign-in screen and post-login permissions checklist.
</Note>

## User flow

1. Sign in with your Voxas account.
2. Review required permissions.
3. Grant microphone and system audio access.

<Note>Image placeholder: macOS microphone permission prompt.</Note>

## How to do it

### Step 1: Sign in

Open Voxas and complete the sign-in flow in the app window.

### Step 2: Open permissions checks

After sign-in, review the permission status shown in the setup screen.

### Step 3: Grant required permissions

Approve microphone access and any other required prompts. If prompted to open System Settings, follow the deep link and enable access.

## What success looks like

- All required permission checks show as complete.
- You can proceed to create or enter a session.

## What could go wrong

### Issue: Permission prompt never appears

- **Why it happens:** macOS may already store a denied state.
- **How to fix:** Open macOS Privacy settings for microphone and enable Voxas manually.

### Issue: Permission appears enabled but audio is still silent

- **Why it happens:** macOS TCC permission state may be stale.
- **How to fix:** Follow the reset steps in [Microphone permissions troubleshooting](/troubleshooting/microphone-permissions).
