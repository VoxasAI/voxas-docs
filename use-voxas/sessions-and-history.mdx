## When to use this

Use this page when you want to resume prior work, compare sessions, or keep a structured history.

</Note>

## User flow

1. Open Sessions from the sidebar.
2. Select an existing session or create a new one.
3. Continue work from the session room.

## How to do it

### Step 1: Browse sessions

Use the sessions list to scan recent and older sessions.

### Step 2: Open the session you need

Click a session to open it in the session room.

### Step 3: Continue your work

Use chat and any available workflow tools to continue from prior context.

## What success looks like

- Session history is visible in one list.
- Opening a session restores its context and timeline.

## What could go wrong

### Issue: A session is missing

- **Why it happens:** Session filter or account mismatch.
- **How to fix:** Verify account and refresh the sessions list.

### Issue: Session opens with stale data

- **Why it happens:** Temporary sync issue.
- **How to fix:** Restart the app and reopen the session.
